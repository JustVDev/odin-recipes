Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are classic, homemade treats loved by people of all ages. These cookies are characterized by their soft and chewy texture and the delightful combination of sweet chocolate and buttery dough.

The dough is made by combining ingredients such as flour, sugar, butter, and eggs. The star of the show is the chocolate chips, which are scattered throughout the dough, providing bursts of chocolatey goodness with every bite. Optionally, you can add chopped nuts or other flavor-enhancing ingredients to customize your cookies.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate chips
  • Optional: nuts or other ingredients

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Cream butter and sugar
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract
  4. Mix in dry ingredients
  5. Add chocolate chips (and nuts, if desired)
  6. Bake the cookies
