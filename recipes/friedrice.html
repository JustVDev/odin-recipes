Fried Rice

Description:

For a delicious homemade fried rice that's bursting with flavor, start by cooking rice according to package instructions and letting it cool completely. In a large skillet or wok, heat oil over medium-high heat and add diced vegetables such as onions, carrots, peas, and bell peppers. Stir-fry the vegetables until they're tender-crisp, then push them to the side of the skillet and add beaten eggs. Scramble the eggs until they're fully cooked, then add the cooked rice to the skillet along with soy sauce and any desired seasonings. Stir-fry everything together until the rice is heated through and evenly coated with the sauce. Serve hot as a delicious side dish or add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a complete meal.

This homemade fried rice recipe is a versatile and satisfying dish that's perfect for using up leftover rice and vegetables. With its vibrant colors and bold flavors, it's sure to become a favorite go-to recipe for quick and easy meals that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • Rice (cooked and cooled) 3 cups
  • Vegetables (diced) 1 cup
  • Eggs 2, beaten
  • Soy Sauce 2-3 tablespoons
  • Oil (for cooking) 2 tablespoons

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions and let it cool completely. In a large skillet or wok, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add 1 cup of diced vegetables such as onions, carrots, peas, and bell peppers to the skillet. Stir-fry the vegetables until they're tender-crisp, then push them to the side of the skillet.
  3. Add 2 beaten eggs to the skillet and scramble until fully cooked. Then, add 3 cups of cooked and cooled rice to the skillet along with 2-3 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  4. Stir-fry everything together until the rice is heated through and evenly coated with the sauce. Serve hot as a side dish or add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu for a complete meal.
