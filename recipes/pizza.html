Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved Italian dish that has gained popularity worldwide. It starts with a base of thin, crispy dough that is topped with a vibrant tomato sauce. The sauce serves as the foundation for an endless variety of toppings, including cheese, vegetables, and various meats.

The cheese, typically mozzarella, is spread generously over the sauce, creating a gooey and stretchy layer that adds richness to every bite. Additionally, pizza can be personalized with toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, olives, or even pineapple, allowing for infinite flavor combinations.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Make the tomato sauce
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add toppings and cheese
  5. Bake the pizza
